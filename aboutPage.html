About Me

I'm captivated by design's ability to evoke emotions, and this passion has led me to embark on a transformative journey to become a designer. The experience has been a continuous, overwhelming, and exhilarating learning process. While I acknowledge that I am still on the learning curve, it's precisely this dynamic nature of the field that draws me in - design is a constant evolution, demanding continuous research and learning.

I have a Bachelor's degree in Media & Cultural Stuides and Sociology from the University of California, Riverside. With my diverse education, I've got a mindful understanding of how users behave. This helps me create designs that are not just inclusive but also connect with the varied dynamics in our society. I also come from a background in education as a substitute teacher and healthcare as a caregiver.
